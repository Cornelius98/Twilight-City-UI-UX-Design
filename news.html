TRENDING IN NG'ONA WARD

Welcome to the official news feed for Ng'ona Ward in Kawambwa District of Zambia. We will keep you updated of any latest development in our community on this platform.

Army urges candidates not to attend drills if they’re sick

HE Zambia Army says special forces recruited candidate Elisha Daka aged 21 collapsed during the mandatory medical military requirement run and was pronounced dead upon arrival at 1Commando Unit Camp hospital.

Cabinet moves to revise age of marriage from 16 to 19

CABINET has approved the publication and introduction in Parliament of the Marriage Amendment Bill, 2023 which intends to revise the age for a void marriage from 16 to 19 years. And Cabinet has also approved the Judicial Training Institute of Zambia Bill, 2023.

Mswati to grace International Trade Fair

FOREIGN Affairs and International Cooperation Minister Stanley Kakubo says King Mswati III of the Kingdom of Eswatini is expected in the country on a state visit from 30th June to 2nd July, 2023. I

ZBF sweats over funds to send teams for tourney

THE Zambia Basketball Federation (ZBF) is sweating for funds to send a team of under 16 boys and girls to the Africa Basketball Championship (ABC) that will be held in Tunisia.

Lungu gives us hope, PF still depends on him – Bowman

PF central committee member Bowman Lusambo says the former ruling party is still dependent on Edgar Lungu because he is still the “father of the party”.

There’s no police brutality, insists IG

POLICE Inspector General Graphael Musamba has insisted that there is no police brutality in the country, urging those who claim they were assaulted by his officers to produce medical reports. On Monday, Musamba said police were very professional and he asked the opposition to tone down and cooperate
